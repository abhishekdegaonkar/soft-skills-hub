Stress Management

Stress management is the practice of controlling stress levels to maintain productivity and well-being. Everyone experiences stress, but learning how to handle it effectively leads to a healthier lifestyle and improved focus.

Common Techniques:

  • Time management and prioritization
  • Regular physical activity
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Healthy lifestyle choices (diet, sleep, hydration)
  • Seeking support from friends or mentors

Why It Matters:

Proper stress management improves decision-making, relationships, and overall happiness. It helps prevent burnout and promotes resilience in the face of challenges.
