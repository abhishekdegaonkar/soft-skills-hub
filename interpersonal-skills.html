Interpersonal Skills

Interpersonal skills are the abilities we use to interact effectively with others. These include communication, empathy, conflict resolution, and emotional awareness. Strong interpersonal skills are vital in both personal and professional life.

Core Components:

  • Active listening
  • Clear communication
  • Respect and empathy
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Positive body language

How to Improve:

Engage in conversations openly, ask questions, give constructive feedback, and work on understanding others’ perspectives.
