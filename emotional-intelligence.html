Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EI) is the ability to understand, manage, and use emotions positively to communicate and build relationships.

Why is it Important?

  • Improves relationships and empathy.
  • Helps manage stress and conflict.
  • Boosts leadership and teamwork.

Core Components

  • Self-awareness
  • Self-regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social skills

How to Improve?

  • Practice mindfulness and self-reflection.
  • Develop empathy by listening actively.
  • Control impulsive reactions.
  • Provide and accept constructive feedback.

Exercise

Write about a recent time you felt strong emotions. How did you react? How could you handle it better next time?
