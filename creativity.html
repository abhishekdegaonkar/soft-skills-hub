Creativity

What is Creativity?

Creativity is the ability to generate new, original, and valuable ideas. It is the foundation of innovation and problem-solving.

Why is it Important?

  • Drives innovation and progress.
  • Helps solve complex problems.
  • Boosts adaptability and critical thinking.

Ways to Boost Creativity

  • Brainstorm freely without judgment.
  • Read and explore new topics.
  • Collaborate with diverse people.
  • Practice creative hobbies (writing, art, music).

Exercise

Choose an everyday object (like a bottle or pen). Write down 5 creative new uses for it.
