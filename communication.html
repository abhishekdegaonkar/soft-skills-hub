Communication Skills

What is Communication?

Communication is the process of sharing information, ideas, or feelings between people. It can be verbal, non-verbal, written, or visual. Effective communication builds trust, reduces misunderstandings, and strengthens relationships.

Why is it Important?

  • Boosts confidence and clarity in expressing ideas.
  • Helps in teamwork and collaboration.
  • Essential for leadership and career growth.
  • Improves conflict resolution and problem-solving.

Types of Communication

  • Verbal: Speaking and listening effectively.
  • Non-Verbal: Body language, gestures, eye contact.
  • Written: Emails, reports, messages.
  • Visual: Charts, presentations, infographics.

How to Improve?

  • Listen actively without interrupting.
  • Maintain good eye contact and posture.
  • Use simple and clear language.
  • Ask questions to confirm understanding.
  • Practice empathy and emotional awareness.

Practice Exercise

Write a short 2-minute speech introducing yourself as if you are in a job interview. Focus on clarity, confidence, and positive body language.
