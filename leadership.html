Leadership Skills

What is Leadership?

Leadership is the ability to guide, inspire, and influence others towards achieving goals.

Why is it Important?

  • Builds trust and motivates team members.
  • Helps manage conflicts and challenges.
  • Creates vision and direction for growth.

Key Qualities of a Leader

  • Integrity and honesty
  • Decision-making skills
  • Empathy and communication
  • Accountability

How to Improve?

  • Practice active listening with your team.
  • Lead by example and demonstrate honesty.
  • Encourage collaboration and creativity.
  • Accept responsibility for both success and failure.

Exercise

Think of a project you worked on. Write down how you would motivate your team if you were the leader.
