Time Management

What is Time Management?

Time management is the process of planning and controlling how much time to spend on activities to maximize efficiency.

Why is it Important?

  • Increases productivity and reduces stress.
  • Helps balance work and personal life.
  • Improves focus and efficiency.

Techniques

  • Prioritize tasks using the Eisenhower Matrix.
  • Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
  • Use Pomodoro technique for focused work.
  • Avoid multitasking.

Exercise

Make a daily schedule for tomorrow. Write down tasks in order of importance and estimate how much time each task will take.
